Room 01

Interior Stillness

Quiet gestures, private reading, and close observation make this room feel slowed down and intimate.

The first room is organized as a calm sequence instead of a collage of competing text. The paintings sit in a cleaner wall arrangement so the experience feels closer to entering a curated chamber than scrolling past a homepage section.

Room 02

Open Horizon

Brick streets, bleaching fields, storm clouds, and river wind push the gallery outward.

The second room widens into landscape and outlook. The wall plan is more orderly here, so navigation feels intentional and the paintings read as a continuous 2D promenade through Dutch weather, streets, and distance.

Room 03

Public Theater

Guard companies, drapers, banquet tables, and family disorder turn public life into performance.

The final room leans into spectacle and social performance. Heavier compositions and richer color close the gallery with a dramatic, public-facing atmosphere rather than another quiet display wall.
